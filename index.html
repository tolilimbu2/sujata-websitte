About Nazha

Nazhe is chinese animated character. He studies to acquire greater skills to become a demo hunter. when he learns about his prophecy of becoming the destroyer of the world, he unwittingly transforms into his demonic form. In Chinese mythology, Nezha (哪吒) is a precocious teenage deity who serves as a patron saint of young adults. After gestating in his mother's womb for three years and six months, Nezha was born with superhuman strength and the ability to speak. His Chinese myth is based off of the Hindu god, Nalakuvara.
